EXPLORING PARIS

Paris, the city of lights, is known for its art, fashion, and culture. Highlights include the Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and charming cafes.

The beauty of Paris stems from its timeless elegance, evident in its harmonious urban planning, consistent architectural style,

grand monuments like the Eiffel Tower and Notre-Dame, and picturesque landscapes along the Seine River

EXPLORING BUDAPEST

Budapest is special for its unique fusion of history, grand architecture, and vibrant culture, highlighted by its famed thermal baths, unique ruin bars, and the iconic Danube River separating the historic Buda and lively Pest.

Budapest, Hungary’s capital, is bisected by the River Danube. Its 19th-century Chain Bridge connects the hilly Buda district with flat Pest. A funicular runs up Castle Hill to Buda’s Old Town, where the Budapest History Museum traces city life from Roman times onward. Trinity Square is home to 13th-century Matthias Church and the turrets of the Fishermen’s Bastion, which offer sweeping views.
